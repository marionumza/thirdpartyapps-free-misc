Work Anniversary Notification by Email

This module will wish the Employees on their Work Anniversary


Overview

HR departments need not worry to wish their employees for their Birthday. In this module we have included the feature to send an email Birthday notification to the employee.

Features:

  • Adds a cron job to invoke email
  • Adds an email template


Go to: Employee

  • Enter work email
  • Under Service Information, Enter Start Date


Go to: Settings / Email / Template / Work Anniversary Notification For Employee
