What is the difference between broadcast list and group in WhatsApp?

WhatsApp Broadcast. WhatsApp Broadcasts are lists of recipients you can send regular (broadcast) messages to. While this may seem similar to a WhatsApp Group, the major difference is that people cannot see other people in the same Broadcast List (making it far more private and secure).
