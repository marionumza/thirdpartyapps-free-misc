Odoo 15 : Similar Products Suggestion Odoo Apps

This module adds a feature to send an email notification with a list of suggested products to customer when product in sale order line is not available.

Features

Add Similar Products in Suggested Product Tab

User can add similar kind of products in one suggested product tab.

Email Notification to Customer/User

When make a sale order in which a product that is not available at the moment is added in sale order line then one Email notification will be sent to customer/user.
Demo Request Video Tutorial

Suggested Products Tab in Product Variants

User can add similar products in this suggested products tab. These products will be shown in Email.

Email Notification to Customer

When we will make a sale order in which a product that is not available at the moment is added in sale order line then one Email notification will be sent to customer.

Email Notification to User

One more email notification will be sent to sales person to that perticular sale order.
