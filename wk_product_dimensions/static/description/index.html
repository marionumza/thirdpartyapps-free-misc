Odoo Product Dimensions

Record product dimensions and weight with Odoo Product Dimensions.

Odoo Product Dimensions makes admin's work easier. With this module, they can add dimensions and weight of products in their record and cross-verify them for each product they purchase. This module helps the admin by adding another attribute to the product. These attributes provide more details to them about the product.

Purpose of Product Dimensions

You are a bulk seller/wholesaler who buys in bulk, so visit a vendor who shows and gives you sample products whose quality and dimensions are what you want to sell. But when you receive your bulk order, the quality and product dimensions are different. Now, what will you do?

This module will help you keep track of product dimensions and weight (in different UOM). So you can compare any product randomly with the UOM mentioned in the backend. In this way, you can get the product order of correct quality and dimensions.
