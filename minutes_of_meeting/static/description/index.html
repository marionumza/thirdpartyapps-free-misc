Minutes of Meeting & CRM Lead Creation

This module helps to organize meetings and add the meeting minutes. With this app, you will be able to Print and Send the Minutes. Module has the provision to create and track the CRM leads.

Features

  1. Organize meetings from Odoo Calendar
  2. Able to define Meeting Organizer
  3. Able to add Agenda, Action Items and Conclusion for meeting
  4. Allow you to print Meeting Minutes PDF Report
  5. Allow you to send Meeting Minutes to the respective attendees
  6. Integration with CRM Lead, has the provision for generating CRM lead according to the Meeting held.
  7. Easy to track the status of Lead from the Meeting Screen instead of going to CRM Menu

Working

  • Go to 'Calendar' Menu --> Click on 'Meetings' Menu and create a meeting.
  • Schedule a meeting by defining the attendees , Give the start and end date of the Meeting, Enter the Agenda of the meeting
  • On meeting creation, Odoo sends an invitation mail to all defined attendees by default. So attendees can accept/decline the invitation


  • We can add the Action Items and Conclusion as per the meeting

  • You can print the Meeting Minutes in PFD Format, by clicking on the button 'Print'

  • You can also send the Minutes by mail (In PFD Format) to the respective attendees.To do that, Click on 'Email' button, then wizard pops-up will displays with the name of the attendees where you can tag additional contacts if required.
  • Click on 'Send' to send the Minutes.

  • You can view the smart button for creating the Lead in the same form rather than switching to CRM Menu.
