Customer Assets for Field Service Management

This module provides creating of Customer Assets for Field Service Management. These Assets can then be selected on a Field Service Task.

Easily create Customer Assets for Field Service Management
Create Customer Assets for Field Service Management and just select those on Field Service Task. Easily navigate to Field Service Tasks also from the Customer Assets and attach extra documents to the record.

2. Select the related Customer

3. Select the related Product
